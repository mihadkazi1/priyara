Shopping Guidance

Client Queries

We offer robust Home Delivery available across Bangladesh. Estimated delivery takes 2–5 business days, and Cash on Delivery option is fully supported.

Depending on the collection item, we feature Premium Egyptian Cotton lawn and Premium Georgette fabric bases. Each features custom luxury embroidery, comfortable finishes, and is highly soft & breathable.

Exchange is available for eligible cases according to PRIYARA's policy. Simply notify our digital concierge or styling staff within 3 days of delivery.

We recommend hand wash with mild detergents. Do not bleach. Dry in shade and iron at a low temperature setting to maintain structural embroidery shape.
